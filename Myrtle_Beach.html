What Makes Myrtle Beach Special?

Myrtle Beach is a city located in South Carolina. The beach is beautiful and the activities are endless. The main attraction is an area called Broadway at the Beach. Broadway at the Beach has so many things to do! Attractions include shops, restaurants, games, playgrounds, rides, escape rooms, mazes, and even ziplining. My favorite things are the escape rooms, shops, and restaurants. I also love spending time at the beach. We usually go to the beach in the morning. Then we would go to the pool after lunch. For dinner we would go to Broadway at the Beach. You should go to Myrtle Beach too!
