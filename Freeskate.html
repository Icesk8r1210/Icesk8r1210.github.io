Freeskate

What is Freeskate

Freeskate is mostly jumps and spins. Freeskate is also the main event for competitions. Freeskate is also another track that you can test at test sessions. For freeskate, you also have a short and a long program depending on what level you're at. Freeskate, in my opinion, is the hardest. The jumps are really hard. I do like that you always get to learn something new. I prefer jumps over spins. Everyone has a preference, some people like jumps and some people like spins. My favorite jump is the double loop. My favorite spin is either the layback or the sit spin. I really like both of them, it's so hard to pick just one! You should try jumps and spins too.
