What is Walt Dinsey World?

Disney World is a theme park located in Orlando, Florida. Disney World has four different parks. Parks include Animal Kingdom, Epcott, Hollywood Studios, and Magic Kingdom. Disney also has a huge shopping area called Disney Springs. Disney Springs has stores, bars, and restaurants. When the holidays roll around, Disney decorates all of its parks. Whatever holiday it is, is whatever the park will be decorated as. I think that it looks the most beautiful around Christmas. They decorate the castle in Christmas lights. It looks so pretty!
