A Little About Me!

Hello everyone! My name is Emily. I like math, space, and of course, cats! I am a figure skater and I really enjoy it! I am currently working on my Junior Moves In The Field. I was planning to go to regionals this year and compete at the juvenile level. Sadly it was canceled because of the Coronavirus. I can do a double sal, double toe loop, double loop, and a double flip. I am also working on my double lutz and double axel. I'm getting pretty close though.

Well, that's just a little bit about me! Stay tuned to hear about more of my adventures and fun with my friends!
