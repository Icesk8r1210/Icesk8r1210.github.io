Moves in the Field

What is Moves in the Field?

Most people hate moves but I love moves. Moves in the Field is a seieres of tests that you take and try to pass. Each test consists of about six patterns of foot work. You must do them in a certain order. The main things the judges are looking for are power and looking good. Looking good as in, having good upper and lower body control, good leg extensions, ect.

Moves in the field are super fun. It's also a great accomplishment. If you pass all the tests, then you can get special things to buy. My favorite thing is a jacket that says gold medalist on the back. I think that you should try passing all of the Moves in the Field tests too.
